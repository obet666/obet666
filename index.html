Story

Once upon a time in a snail world, living a friendly little snail named Nitro. Nitro lived in a little family. His family was caring and loving, they really wanted nitro to have bright future. When nitro passed the high school, he received a scholar to join one the best Racing School in snail world. His parents were so proud of him, especially his father that really wanted him to join that university. Long story short, Nitro has become a student there. By the time, Nitro started to feel that he chose wrong major. Why Nitro chose that major from the start? Because that was his fathers will. His father was a racer that is why he wanted Nitro to follow his path to be a snail racer. Nitro has no interest at all about snail race, and it made him bullied by Slash and friend because he knew nothing about snail race. Short time later, Nitro's father passed, he gave his last message to his son, Nitro. He said “Sorry for not able to see you become a great snail racer, but I believe that you will be a great snail racer.” The last message from his father became the turning point in Nitro’s life. Since then, he wanted to be the best snail racer in the snail world. A couple years later, Nitro passed the racing school and ready to be a snail racer. Afterward, Nitro and the other registered themselves in a snail race competition. Rivalry and the competition were really strict, but it didn’t make Nitro give up. Long struggle, tiring race and the tense of the competition was passed. In the end, Nitro became the winner of the snail race competition by defeating his rivals. With smile proud, Nitro then said “Look dad, now I am the best snail racer”.
